# Question:
Consider a noiseless channel BW is 3kHz transmitting a signal of  2 signal levels. Find the total capacity of channel ?

## ⭐ Given:
- Bandwidth, **B = 3 kHz = 3000 Hz**
- Signal levels, **L = 2**
- Channel = **Noiseless channel**

---

## ⭐ Formula (Nyquist Theorem):
$$C = 2B \log_2(L)$$

---

## ⭐ Step 1: Substitute values
$$CC = 2 \times 3000 \times \log_2(2)$$

---

## ⭐ Step 2: Solve log term

$$log_2(2) = 1$$

---

## ⭐ Step 3: Final calculation

$$C=2×3000×1=6000 bps$$

---

## ⭐ Final Answer:

✔ **Channel Capacity = 6000 bps (or 6 kbps)**

---

# ⭐ Quick Intuition:

- Bandwidth = 3 kHz → limits speed
- 2 signal levels → only 1 bit per symbol
- Nyquist doubles bandwidth effect → final = 6000 bps
